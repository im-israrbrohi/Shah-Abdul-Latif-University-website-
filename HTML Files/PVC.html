Respected Staff, Faculty, and Students,

I convey my sincere respect and utmost satisfaction in my capacity as the Pro Vice Chancellor of the Shehdadkot Campus at Shah Abdul Latif University Khairpur as I address you today. It has been a tremendous honour to serve in this capacity for the past three years, and I am immensely pleased with the progress that our team has achieved collectively. Since its inception, my overarching goal for Shehdadkot Campus has been to convert it into a symbol of scholarly distinction, a driving force in advancing education in the rural regions of Sindh, and a provider of all-encompassing facilities for research and academia. To foster an atmosphere in which each person can flourish intellectually, academically, and personally, our objective is not merely to meet but to surpass expectations. In pursuit of this objective, we are dedicated to a number of pivotal endeavours.
To begin with, our institution is committed to improving our scholarly programmes by guaranteeing that they are rigorous, pertinent, and attuned to the requirements of contemporary society and our students. Furthermore, we are committed to enhancing our research capacities and cultivating an environment that encourages inventive thinking and investigation, thereby expanding the limits of understanding and making significant societal contributions.
Additionally, I assure you of my utmost dedication to furnishing every essential resource required for the development of a novel campus edifice. By virtue of accommodating our expanding faculty and student body, this facility will also symbolise our dedication to advancement and growth. It is crucial to acknowledge that the accomplishments of this nature would not have been feasible without the combined endeavour and cooperation of all individuals comprising our scholarly community. The commitment and labour of our faculty and staff, whose contributions have been indispensable to our accomplishments to date, have my deepest gratitude. In the future, I strongly advocate for the maintenance of cooperation and collaboration, as it is solely by means of our collective endeavours that we can effect genuine change.
In conclusion, I would like to extend my sincere appreciation to each individual present for their steadfast backing and commitment to the objectives of Shehdadkot Campus. I am certain that, by working together, we will persistently surpass our institution's previous levels of excellence and influence. With warm regards, Wishing you all the best in your academic endeavors


Warm regards,
Dr. Chandan Lal Rohra
Provisional Vice Chancellor
