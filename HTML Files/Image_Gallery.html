Student Life

Welcome to the vibrant student life at Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot Campus! Established in 2017 under the guidance of the Higher Education Commission and the Sindh Government, our campus is dedicated to providing quality education to the community of Shahdadkot.

Step into our state-of-the-art classrooms where learning comes to life. Designed to facilitate interactive sessions and discussions, our classrooms provide a conducive environment for academic excellence. With modern teaching aids and comfortable seating arrangements, you'll find yourself fully engaged in every lecture.

Immerse yourself in a world of knowledge at our expansive library. Housing a vast collection of books, journals, and research papers, our library is the perfect place for academic exploration. Whether you're studying for exams or conducting research for your projects, you'll find all the resources you need right at your fingertips.

Software Expo 2024

Students from Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot campus recently visited the Main Campus in Khairpur to participate in the Software Expo 204. This event showcased innovative projects by final-year students, providing a platform for learning and collaboration.

The initiative signifies progress in addressing the challenge of limited access to quality higher education in the region. With the establishment of new campuses and events like the Software Expo, educational opportunities are expanding. However, challenges such as poverty and resource scarcity persist, hampering access to education in prestigious institutions outside the region.

PM Laptop Scheme 2024

Students at Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot campus recently received laptops through the PM Laptop Scheme 2023-2024. The distribution event held at Main Campus Khairpur was a moment of pride for all.

Final Examination

At Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot Campus, final examinations are conducted with utmost stringency to ensure a fair and honest academic environment. Rigorous measures are implemented to prevent any form of cheating or academic misconduct during exams.

The administration maintains a strict vigilance to uphold the integrity of the examination process, promoting a culture of academic honesty and integrity among students. Additionally, the examination checking process is transparent, guaranteeing fairness and accuracy in the evaluation of students' performance. This commitment to maintaining high standards of academic conduct fosters a conducive learning environment where students can demonstrate their knowledge and skills confidently.

AL- Khidmat Orientation Session

The Al-Khidmat Orientation Session at Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot Campus was a vital initiative focused on raising awareness about the spread of contagious diseases. Through interactive discussions and informative presentations, students were enlightened about the importance of preventive measures and hygiene practices to curb the transmission of illnesses.

The session emphasized the significance of community health and collective responsibility in combating contagious diseases effectively. Attendees gained valuable insights into identifying symptoms, practicing proper sanitation, and promoting overall well-being. Such initiatives play a crucial role in equipping individuals with knowledge and empowering communities to safeguard public health.

AL- Khidmat Medical Camp

Students at Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot Campus enthusiastically participated in a medical camp aimed at providing healthcare services to underserved communities. With compassion and dedication, they offered their support in various capacities, assisting healthcare professionals, organizing logistics, and providing basic medical aid to patients.

Such initiatives embody the university's commitment to community engagement and holistic education.
