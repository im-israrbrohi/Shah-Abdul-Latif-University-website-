Office of Research, Innovation, and Commercialization (ORIC)




About

The Office of Research, Innovation, and Commercialization (ORIC) is dedicated to promoting research excellence, fostering innovation, and facilitating the commercialization of research outcomes.

Mission

Our mission is to advance knowledge through cutting-edge research, drive innovation to address societal challenges, and support the transformation of research findings into tangible solutions that benefit society.

Research Commercialization

ORIC works to bridge the gap between academic research and commercialization by providing resources, support, and expertise to researchers, entrepreneurs, and industry partners interested in bringing innovative ideas to market.

HR Development

We are committed to nurturing talent and developing the next generation of researchers, innovators, and entrepreneurs through training programs, mentorship initiatives, and opportunities for professional development.

Research Support

ORIC offers a wide range of services to support researchers at every stage of the research process, including grant writing assistance, research funding opportunities, research ethics compliance, and intellectual property management.

Objectives

  • Facilitate interdisciplinary research collaborations.
  • Promote innovation and entrepreneurship among faculty and students.
  • Enhance the impact and visibility of research outcomes.
  • Foster a culture of research excellence and integrity.
  • Support the translation of research findings into real-world applications.
