Examination Policy


Examination Pattern

At Shah Abdul Latif University, Shahdadkot Campus, the examination pattern is designed to ensure comprehensive assessment of students' academic progress and performance. The university follows a structured approach, aligning with academic standards and promoting holistic development among students.

Semester Structure:

The academic year comprises two semesters, each characterized by a well-rounded curriculum covering diverse subjects essential for students' academic growth. Within each semester, students undertake a set of five subjects, fostering a balanced learning experience.

Examination Components:

Each semester is further divided into two significant examination components:

Midterm Examinations:

  • Midterm examinations serve as a crucial checkpoint for students, evaluating their understanding and grasp of the subject matter.
  • Each midterm paper carries a weightage of 30 marks, contributing significantly to the overall assessment.
  • The midterms are designed to gauge students' comprehension and analytical skills, laying the groundwork for their academic journey.

Final Examinations:

  • The culmination of the semester, final examinations, encapsulate the entirety of students' learning and knowledge acquisition.
  • Final exams hold a greater weightage, with each paper accounting for 50 marks, reflecting the depth and breadth of students' expertise in the respective subjects.
  • These examinations provide a comprehensive evaluation of students' proficiency and mastery of the course material, ensuring readiness for further academic pursuits.

Additional Evaluation Criteria:

Beyond academic assessments, Shah Abdul Latif University incorporates various parameters to gauge students' overall performance and engagement:

  • Attendance (5 Marks): Regular attendance is integral to academic success, with students earning marks based on their punctuality and participation in classes.
  • Class Activities (5 Marks): Active involvement in class activities and discussions is encouraged, with marks allocated to recognize students' contributions to the learning environment.
  • Assignments (20 Marks): Assignments serve as a platform for students to demonstrate their understanding of the course material, with substantial weightage allocated to assess their effort and comprehension.

Paper Rechecking Policy

At Shah Abdul Latif University Shahdadkot campus, the process of paper rechecking is meticulously conducted to ensure accuracy and fairness in grading. Students who wish to request a reevaluation of their examination papers can do so by paying a nominal fee of 250 per paper. This fee contributes to the administrative costs associated with the rechecking process. Upon submission of a rechecking request, papers are thoroughly reviewed by experienced faculty members to identify any errors or discrepancies in grading..
