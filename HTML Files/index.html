About Us

As Shahdadkot campus of SALU established in 2017 and has been working under the kind supervision of Higher Education Commission and Sindh Government that is rendering educational services to the masses of shahdadkot. We want to appreciate your steps regarding the uplift of education In hard area where we had been observing the threat of criminal upsurge in district Qambar Shahdadkot

The reason behind this was only lack of quality education or unavailability of higher educational institutions in the area. Now, in 2020 many things have been improved due to courageous step o f HEC for opening a campus. On the other hand people of this region are being faced with poverty, lack of resources and are unable to seek admission in far-flung institutions like, Sindh University, Mehran University, LUMÎ—S, NED and University of Karachi.

In this regard Civil Society of shahdadkot, Media and responsible figures have appreciated the efforts of HEC regarding enhancement of the quality education at shahdadkot with easy access to all communities by development of campus. Further, the poor students of Balochistan are availing this facility because shahdadkot is covering the large adjacent border areas of Baluchistan also. In this regard we hope from HEC that by observing the needs of quality education for the people of shahdadkot, and Balochistan HEC would f urther expedite the process of funding.

Keeping in view the current scenario because the present building of STEVETA where campus is running now a days is in deplorable and life threatening condition. Hence, HEC is requested to increase the funding that the new campus may be constructed at the donated site at CPEC Road or may transferred at another suitable place.

Programs Offered

Computer Science

Computer Science is a dynamic field that encompasses the study of algorithms, data structures, programming languages, software engineering, artificial intelligence, and much more.

Students in the Computer Science program gain hands-on experience in coding, problem-solving, and software development, preparing them for careers in technology-driven industries.

Business Administration

Business Administration is a versatile field that covers various aspects of management, finance, marketing, human resources, and organizational behavior.

Students in the Business Administration program learn essential skills for leadership, decision-making, strategic planning, and entrepreneurship, enabling them to thrive in diverse business environments.

English Language and Literature

English Language and Literature explores the intricacies of language, literature, and communication, examining texts from different periods, cultures, and genres.

Students in the English Language and Literature program develop critical thinking, analytical, and communication skills, preparing them for careers in writing, teaching, publishing, media, and more.
